# Expense Tracker App

A **Flutter-based Expense Tracker App** to manage your **income, expenses, budgets**, view **reports**, and monitor your **financial analytics**. Fully integrated with **Firebase Authentication** and **Firestore**.

---

## ðŸ”¹ Features

- **User Authentication**: Login/Signup using Email & Password.
- **Dashboard**: Quick overview of total income, total expenses, and remaining budget.
- **Income & Expenses Management**:
  - Add, update, delete income/expenses.
  - Category-wise list.
- **Budget Management**: Set monthly budgets and track usage.
- **Reports**: View expenses by category and date.
- **Analytics**: Summary cards for income, expenses, savings, and budget usage.
- **Profile Management**: View user info fetched from Firebase.
- **Notifications**: Alerts for expenses, budgets, and reminders.
- **Settings**: Update app preferences.
- **Help & About**: FAQs and app info.
- **Professional UI**: Modern gradients, rounded cards, and clean design.
